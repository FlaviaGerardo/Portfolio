Summary

Builted an skin app that detects your type of skin and suggest you the right creams and treatments.

Briefing

The Wellness Institute is very excited to explore how they can leverage technology to help people live a healthier life.

The institute defines wellness as an active process through which people become aware of, and make choices toward, a more successful existence.

Knowing our public & sourching needs

TAfter a lot of enterviews and surveys, I’ve discoverd that useres needs are not fullfield at the marked.

They would like to have an App that photographs their skin and tell them more about their type and needs, suggest them about products and have rattings and comments about them. Beside that, they want to have prices comparation between shops.

62% of the users needs to know their skin type.

52% of the users wants ratting and comentaries about the products.

49% wants the option to photograph their skin. .

30% wants the one feauture that compares prices in different shops.

Knowing about User Journey

Wild Ideas Gone Wild

The next phase was really fun. I’ve created a new brand called “Simply Skin”, and started drawing the new App according to the user needs.

Value Propousal

I’ve believed that by making an app where they can photograph their skin and give them recommendations of suitable products, as well as comparison between similar products, price comparison and ratings/feedback from other users, all in the same place, users will feel more insurance and we will make them learn more about their skin and buy more. We'll know we're right when we get users to use those features and feel safe without having to go to a dermatologist (if there are no major problems), make purchases and comment in the app.
